Autumn Moon

This logo, designed for a personal brand celebrating the Chinese Autumn Moon Festival, elegantly blends minimalism with cultural symbolism. The design features a sleek, golden outline of a fish, subtly hinting at the traditional imagery associated with the festival, such as abundance and good fortune. The fish’s eye is shaped like a crescent moon, symbolizing the festival’s focus on the full moon. The use of gold against a dark background conveys a sense of elegance and sophistication. The brand name, "Autumn Moon," is rendered in a modern font, with the word "Moon" highlighted in gold to draw attention and create visual harmony with the fish outline. This logo effectively captures the essence of the Autumn Moon Festival while maintaining a contemporary and stylish appeal.
